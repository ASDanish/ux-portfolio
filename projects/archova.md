# ARCHOVA — Architectural Visualization Studio

**Type:** UX case study (Figma) · **Scope:** Marketing website

A website for an architectural visualization studio — selling 3D rendering, modeling, and animation services to architects and designers worldwide.

## Highlights

- Full-bleed interior photography paired with a dark, editorial layout.
- Service pillars stated up front: Stunning Visualizations, Precision Modeling, Dynamic Animations, Client Collaboration.
- A "Creative Projects" section listing real project types (Casa A.R, Botanical Garden, Penthouse) to ground the studio's capability in concrete work.
- Newsletter capture and a "We work with the best brands" trust section before the footer.
