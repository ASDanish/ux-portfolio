# Ambur First

**Type:** Shipped product (in development) · **Scope:** Mobile/desktop app (Flutter)

A hyperlocal community app for the town of Ambur — "Everything happening in Ambur, all in one place." A single hub for local news, listings, and community activity in a town not otherwise well served by mainstream apps.

![Ambur First splash screen](../assets/ambur-first/hero.jpg)

## Notes

- Built with Flutter, targeting cross-platform (mobile + desktop) delivery from one codebase.
- Screen shown is the app's launch/splash screen; the app is still under active development.
